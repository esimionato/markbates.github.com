--- layout: page title: features tags: [] status: draft type: page published: false meta: _edit_last: '1' _wp_page_template: default --- Mack is a Ruby web application framework that is being built from the ground up to aid in the development of distributed, portal-like applications that have a need for speed. Mack supports best of breed technologies in a modular way.
  • ORM Agnostic - Use DataMapper, ActiveRecord, Sequel, or "roll your own"! There are already gems to support DataMapper and ActiveRecord, more are on their way.
  • Templating language agnostic. Support for ERB (Erubis), Xml::Builder, and Markaby are there and ready to go. But it's easy to add your own.
