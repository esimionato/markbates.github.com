So, to that end there are varies ways to get support for your mack application. If you'd like to log a bug ticket you can go to Lighthouse to do so: http://lighthouse.mackframework.com For everything else there's the mailing list: http://groups.google.com/group/mack-framework For those who prefer forums to mailing lists there's: http://www.mackforum.com If you're an IRC fan, then head over to irc.freenode.net and head to #mackframework. If all else fails, then bugs@mackframework.com will definitely get you the answers you need.
